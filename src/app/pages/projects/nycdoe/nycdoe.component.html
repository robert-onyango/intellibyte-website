New York City Department of Education (NYC DoE)

The NYC DoE is the largest public school system in the U.S., serving over 1 million students in more than 1,800 schools across all five boroughs.

Objective

Rewrite the application to showcase cloud-native design and implementation strategies, and evaluate vendor software solutions.

Timeline and Milestones

August 2022

  • Assessed features and capabilities of the Student Parent Leader Application.
  • Identified technologies and obtained stakeholder buy-in for the MVP solution.

September 2022

  • Initiated collaborative development using ASP.NET Core 6, Angular 14, and SQL Server.
  • Employed cloud-native architectures and TDD practices.
  • Conducted weekly update meetings with stakeholders.

October 2022

  • Hosted NYC DoE technical staff for workshops on cloud-native frameworks.
  • Demonstrated application architecture to aid in vendor evaluation and in-house development.

November 2022

  • Finalized application development and presented to the entire IT department.
  • Assessed vendor applications and provided comprehensive evaluation guidelines.

Outcomes and Achievements

  • Successfully showcased cloud-native design, improving performance and UI.
  • Integrated cutting-edge technologies enhancing robustness and reliability.
  • Improved application responsiveness by over 90%.
  • Provided workshops empowering NYC DoE team in vendor evaluation and development.
  • Left detailed guidelines for evaluating external software solutions.
  • Equipped the department with knowledge for future development tasks.
